Tony Stark, a billionaire genius and weapons manufacturer, was kidnapped by terrorists who forced him to build a weapon. Instead, he constructed a powered exoskeleton suit to escape. Publication history

Premiere

Iron Man is a fictional superhero appearing in Marvel Comics, created by writer Stan Lee, developed by Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 (1963).


Solo Series

Iron Man is one of Marvels most iconic superheroes, known for his intelligence, wit, and leadership in the Avengers. His sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame marked the culmination of his arc, making him one of the most beloved characters in the MCU.

Fictional Character biography

However, his distant and demanding nature often left Tony seeking his approval.
